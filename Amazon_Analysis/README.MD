#  Amazon Sales Analysis (SQL Project)

##  Project Overview

This project analyzes Amazon-style sales data using **SQL** to uncover key business insights such as revenue trends, customer behavior, regional performance, and operational efficiency.

The goal is to demonstrate how raw transactional data can be transformed into **actionable insights for business decision-making**.

---

##  Business Objectives

* Track **monthly sales trends**
* Identify **high-performing regions and categories**
* Analyze **order cancellations and revenue loss**
* Evaluate **sales agent performance**
* Understand **customer purchasing behavior**

---

##  Dataset Description

The dataset consists of transactional sales data with the following key attributes:

* Order ID
* Order Date
* Customer ID
* Region (North, South, East, West)
* Product Name & Category
* Quantity & Unit Price
* Total Amount
* Order Status (Delivered, Pending, Cancelled, Shipped)
* Sales Agent

##  Key Analysis & Queries

###  Monthly Sales Trend

* Analyzed revenue over time (Year & Month)
* Helps identify **seasonality and growth trends**

---

###  Cancellation & Pending Rate by Region

* Calculated percentage of unsuccessful orders
* Identifies **operational inefficiencies**

---

###  Revenue Loss Analysis

* Measured revenue lost due to cancellations
* Highlighted **top 3 loss-making regions**

---

###  Average Order Value (AOV)

* Calculated per category
* Helps understand **customer spending behavior**

---

###  Top Performing Sales Agents

* Ranked agents based on revenue generated
* Useful for **performance evaluation**

---

###  Category Contribution

* Percentage contribution of each category to total sales
* Helps identify **high-value product segments**

---

###  Customer Behavior Analysis

* Identified customers with multiple orders
* Useful for **loyalty and retention strategies**

---

##  Key Insights

*  Certain regions consistently generate higher revenue
*  Some regions have higher cancellation/pending rates
*  Electronics category contributes the most to revenue
*  Top sales agents significantly impact total sales
*  Repeat customers play an important role in revenue generation

---

##  Business Recommendations

* Improve logistics in **high cancellation regions**
* Focus marketing on **top-performing categories**
* Reward **high-performing sales agents**
* Target repeat customers with **loyalty programs**
